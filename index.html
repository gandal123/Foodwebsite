Gulab Jamun

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Baking powder
  • Flour
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire. Add cardamom and keep asi.
  2. In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji,Milk, baking powder and baking soda.
  3. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  4. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns.
  5. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup.
  6. Once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame.
  7. Serve warm

Peda

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Corn flour
  • Lemon juice
  • Ghee
  • Elaichi Powder

Recipe

  1. Mix together all the ingredients except elaichi powder.
  2. Heat the mixture in a heavy bottom kadai.
  3. Cook with constant stirring till thick and the mixture starts leaving the sides of the kadai.
  4. Remove from fire and cool.
  5. Shape into pedas and sprinkle with elaichi powder.

Kheer

Ingredient

  • Milk
  • Dry fruits
  • Rice
  • Elaichi Powder

Recipe

  1. Wash rice and pressure cook it in milk for 10 minutes.
  2. Add milk and cook for another 5 to 7 minutes, stirring constantly, until the kheer reaches the desired consistency.
  3. Remove from the flame.
  4. Serve garnished with nuts and/or elaichi powder.
